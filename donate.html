Support Us

Columbia University Ballet Ensemble provides our dancers the experience of dancing a high caliber production of a full-length classical story ballet which would not be possible without the support of our generous donors. While we are actively fundraising and applying for grants, providing our dancers the opportunity to perform in a professional theater with elegant costumes and sets does not come without a financial cost. CUBE pays the rental expense for a professional midtown theater and is continually updating costumes and props, making posters and programs, hiring photographers, providing snacks to dancers during in-theater rehearsals, and more.


Your support would be extremely valuable. No amount is too small. The three levels of donation are outlined below:


1. Corps de Ballet (under $200): Corps de Ballet donors receive recognition in our program.


2. Soloist ($200-$499): Soloist donors receive an invitation to our dress rehearsal and recognition in our program.


3. Principal ($500 and above): Principal donors receive two complimentary tickets to one of our shows for that semester, an invitation to our dress rehearsal, and recognition in our program.
